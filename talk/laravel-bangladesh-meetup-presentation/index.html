Laravel Bangladesh Meetup 1.0 Presentation

20 November, 2017

Recently(18th November, 2017) I have attended the first ever Laravel meetup of Bangladesh. It was held at World University of Bangladesh(WUB) Panthapath Campus. Around 330 people attended the meetup. 4 speaker presented their talk at the meetup. Our venue partner was WUB and sponsor was Techmasters.

The title of my speech was High Performance Laravel, was basically about how you can optimize Laravel for handle high traffic website and APIs. Here is the slides of that speech. Enjoy.
